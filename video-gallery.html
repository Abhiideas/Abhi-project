Stories of Impact | How your help is reaching to the most needy.

Food Drive Outside Hospitals

Uday Foundation serve free, but wholesome, food to families of underprivileged patients in the Govt. Hospital and also for the children who come from a far-off places to the Delhi hospitals for often longer treatments.

Winter Drive Outside Hospitals

Every winter, Our Foundation runs Blanket Donation Campaign,

Stories of Impact | How your help reached to the victims of natural disasters.

Uttarakhand Floods

In June 2013, Uttarakhand faced devastating floods, which was India's worst natural disaster since a decade. Our Foundation SOS response teams reached the remote flood affected areas with essential help. We still continue to work in Uttarakhand with various charity programs.

Kashmir Floods

In 2014, Kashmir was hit by devastating floods that shook the entire nation. Uday Foundation was among few organizations to start emergency relief work with in few hours. We continue to work in remote areas of Jammu and Kashmir with the help of Armed Forces.
Indian Automobile Industry in association with Our Foundation Made Donations To Fight COVID 19
TVS Motor Company, TVS Credit Services Ltd, and our Foundation today announced their commitment to donate Rs. 25 Crores to the PM's Relief Fund to aid the battle against COVID-19.

We supported 100 Migrant Workers In Thiruvananthapuram

21 days nationwide lockdown imposed to contain the spread of novel coronavirus is proving to be tough for various sections of the society especially migrant workers. In Kerala, 10 migrant workers living together share their story of surviving the lockdown with little to no money and donations received from non-governmental organisations (NGOs).
