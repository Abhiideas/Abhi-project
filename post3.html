Don't let them sleep hungry for another day, donate a ration kit

Our Foundation will add 25% to your donation up to Rs.10,000*. If you donate Rs.1,000, Rs.250 will be added, which in turn provides more ration to families facing lockdown hunger - Donate now and multiply your impact.

Millions of poor labourers are struggling to access food and rations during the COVID lockdown to keep themselves and their families safe. All it takes is Rs.500 to give one family a week's worth basic food essentials. Donate the money that you have saved on conveyance or on that cuppa at your favourite cafe to these marginalised communities. Help them with ration kits.

GiveIndia through Mission: Ration Kit for Families Facing Lockdown Hunger aims to provide 70,000 ration kits to the poor families who are penniless due to the lockdown.

Each weekly ration kit costs Rs.500 and contains daal, rice, salt, oil, atta, masala, tea, sugar etc. These kits will be distributed in areas like Maharashtra, parts of Rajasthan, Bihar and Tamil Nadu.

*Our Foundation's 25% top-up is capped at Rs.2,500. So to a donation of Rs.10,000, Rs.2,500 will be added. To all donations above Rs.10,000 also Rs.2,500 will be added.
