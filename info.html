Symptoms of Corona Virus

fever, dry cough, tiredness, diarrhoea
conjunctivitis, loss of taste or smell
difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
rashes on skin, sore throat, headache
chest pain or pressure,aches and pains
discolouration of fingers or toes

Precautions

Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or sanitizers
Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing
Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible
Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth
If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention
